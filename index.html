Unnecessarily bad design

  • Deletion confirmation

    Have you regretted answering “Really delete this?” with yes?

  • Door handles

    Have you pushed a door that should be pulled?

  • Microwave ovens

    Does it take more time to set up the machine than to warm your food?

  • Radiator valves

    Do you turn your radiator to its highest setting to heat the room faster?

  • Snooze buttons

    Do you stay in bed five minutes longer for half an hour?

  • Stove knobs

    Do you keep accidentally turning on the wrong plate on your stove top?

  • Traffic lights

    Are you anxious driving towards a green light that it might turn red soon?

If you know more examples of unnecessarily bad design, contribute to the GitHub repository.
